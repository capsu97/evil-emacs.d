**** Tips and tricks
To insert a newline use C-j.
Use C-' to hide all lines without a cursor. Press C-' again to unhide.
Using mc/mark-next-like-this without region will add a cursor on the next line.
Try mc/mark-all-like-this-dwim on tagname in html-mode.
You can use pos or neg prefix with mc/edit-lines to change how it behaves on too short lines.
